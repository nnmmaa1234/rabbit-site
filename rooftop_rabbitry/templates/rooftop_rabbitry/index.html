A little about us

Here at the Rooftop Rabbitry, we are dedicated to providing the best quality rabbits for you. We may not have a variety of breeds, but we sure have a variety of colors to choose from, i.e. Black, White, Tort, Brown.. We also have a variety of ages to choose from. We have rabbits that are ready to breed, and we have rabbits that are ready to be your family pet. We also have rabbits that are ready to be your next show rabbit. We have the Dwarf Hotots, Flemish Giants and Holland Lops.

How we started

​In 2019 we bought our first pair of New Zealand bunnies and fell in love! Shortly after we also started breeding Dwarf Hotots. Rabbits have been therapeutic pets to our family and we adore all their personalities! It's been an amazing journey with these buns and we are dedicated daily to providing the best care possible for our little furry friends! All our animals are fed natural Alfa Alfa Hay, as well as commercial pellets.

We are blessed to live here in the city of Lahore, that is the captial of the Punjaab, in Pakistan! We are a small family that decided to open a rabbitry on our rooftop where me and my children raise bunnies as well as fish. We are located in Valencia town. Pretty much the sub-urbs of Lahore .

We try to provide the best care to our bunnies with nutritional food, a clean, and cool environment.
