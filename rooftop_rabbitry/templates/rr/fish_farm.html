Micropoecilia Picta (Swamp Guppies)

Micropoecilia Picta, also known as Swamp Guppies, are a small freshwater fish species found in South America. They are known for their colorful and vibrant appearance, with males displaying bright blue and red coloration. They are relatively easy to care for and can be kept in community aquariums, although they prefer densely planted environments with lots of hiding places.
